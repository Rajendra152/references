Two Way ANOVA Report Graphs

You can generate up to seven graphs using the results from a Two Way ANOVA. They include a:

  • Histogram of the residuals.
  • Normal probability plot of the residuals.
  • 3D plot of the residuals.
  • Grouped bar chart of the column means.
  • 3D category scatter plot.
  • Multiple comparison graphs.
  • Profile plots.
