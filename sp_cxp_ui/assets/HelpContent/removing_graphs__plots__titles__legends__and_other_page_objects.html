Removing Graphs, Plots, Titles, Legends, and Other Page Objects

Anything on the graph page can be removed from the page by selecting the object, then pressing Delete.

Deleting removes curves, plots, and graphs entirely. You can use undo (Ctrl+Z) to retrieve these items. When a graph or plot is removed, worksheet data remains intact. Delete also completely removes drawn and pasted objects.

Note: Delete only hides titles and legends, and does not remove them permanently.
