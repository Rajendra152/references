Main Effects

Main Effects graphs are available for the following tests:

  • Two Way Analysis of Variance (ANOVA).
  • Three Way Analysis of Variance (ANOVA).

For Main Effects, there is one plot per graph and the number of graphs equals the number of factors. For each graph, the levels of one factor are fixed, while cell means are averaged over all levels of the other factors (one other factor for Two-Way ANOVA, two other factors for Three-Way ANOVA).
