Locking Legend Text

Locking legends halts all automatic updating of the legend text for the whole legend. For example, if you lock the legend, you can change column titles and column data without resetting the legend label. The legend will automatically update, however, if you remove or add a curve.

You can also lock a legend by editing it.

If you do not lock the legend, the legend automatically updates itself when you change column titles and data. Locking the legend affects the entire legend, not just individual entries.

To lock legend text:

  1. Double-click the graph to open Graph Properties.
  2. In the Properties list, select Graph > Legends.
  3. Under Legend properties select Lock legend.
