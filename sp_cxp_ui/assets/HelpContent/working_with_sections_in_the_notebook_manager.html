Working with Sections in the Notebook Manager

Notebook sections are placeholders in the notebook. They contain notebook items, but no data; however, you can name, open, and close notebook sections.

You can create as many new sections as you want in a notebook. You may also create reports within each section to document the items in each section.

To expand or collapse a section, double-click the section icon or click the Up or Right arrows.
